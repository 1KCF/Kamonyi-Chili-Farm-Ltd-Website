Chili Product

Green Chili 

Green chili is immature chili pepper, most often Pasilla, Anaheim or Poblano peppers that have been harvested before fully ripening.

Bird Eye Chili 

Bird eye chili plant is a perennial with small, tapering fruits, often two or three, at a node. The fruits are very pungent.

Fresh Chili

Fresh chili pepper from Nahuatl chili is the fruit of plants from the genus Capsicum which are members of the nightshade family, Solanaceae. Chili peppers are widely used in many cuisines as a spice to add heat to dishes,

About KCF Ltd

Vision of KCF Ltd:

​

Kamonyi Chili Farm (KCF) aims to becoming everyone first choice by adherence to international standards and empower smallholder chili farmers. We want to significantly improve the lives of over 1 Million farmers in Rwanda and in the long run help farmers in from all over the world by increasing their harvest, optimizing their activities and finally increasing their revenues empowers single mothers’ farmers through outreach and training. Moreover, we want to work with chili producers and cooperatives where we will focus on training them so that we can have the same quality.

​

Mission of KCF Ltd:

​

The main mission of Kamonyi Chili Farm (KCF) is to add value of Chili product, increase the consumption of Chili, and increase its quantity even during the dry season.
